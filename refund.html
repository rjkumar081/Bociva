Refund Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

This policy outlines the conditions under which a refund may be processed for services provided by MeraPopi Digital Seva Center.

Service Fee Policy (Non-Refundable)

Our fees are for our **professional services**, which include consultation, document verification, application submission, and follow-up. Our service fee is **non-refundable** once the work has been initiated, which typically occurs immediately upon receipt of payment and documents.

**Example:** If you pay for GST registration and we begin data entry, the service fee is considered earned, even if the government rejects the application later.

Government/Third-Party Fees (Case-by-Case)

Fees paid to third parties (e.g., government application fees, IRCTC booking charges, visa application fees) are governed by their respective policies. If the official entity issues a refund for those specific charges, we will promptly process and remit the refund amount back to you, minus any bank charges.

Non-Refundable Scenarios

A refund will **not** be issued under the following circumstances:

  • The application is rejected, delayed, or cancelled by the government or third-party provider.
  • The user provides incomplete, incorrect, or fraudulent documentation.
  • The user cancels the request after our service work has commenced.
  • For travel bookings, if the cancellation is outside the permissible window of the airline, railway, or hotel policy.

Review Request

To request a review for a potential refund (only applicable to official fees or cases of proven error on our part), please email us at **support@merapopi.com** within **7 days** of the transaction, providing your invoice and a detailed explanation of the issue.
