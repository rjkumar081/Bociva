Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

The security and privacy of Your Personal Data is our utmost priority. This Privacy Policy describes how MeraPopi Digital Seva Center collects, uses, and protects your information.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect only the Personal Data necessary to fulfill the services you request. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • **Contact Information:** Name, address, phone number, email address.
  • **Sensitive Information:** Aadhaar Number, PAN Number, Passport details, and other government-issued IDs required for application processing.

How Your Data is Used

Your Personal Data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • **Service Fulfillment:** To fill out and submit your application forms to the respective official government portals (e.g., UIDAI, Income Tax Dept., Passport Authority) or third-party booking agents (e.g., IRCTC, airlines).
  • **Communication:** To update you on the status of your application and respond to your queries.
  • **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

Data Security and Confidentiality

We employ modern security measures, including encrypted transmission and controlled access, to protect your data. Your sensitive information is **never sold or rented** to any third parties for marketing purposes. It is only shared with the official entities required to process your service request.

Payment Safety

All online payments are processed through secure, industry-standard gateways. We do not store your credit card or sensitive financial details on our servers.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, You can contact us at **support@merapopi.com**.
